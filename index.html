Himti Unesa

we are a student organization majoring in informatics engineering at the state university of surabaya (UNESA). himti was founded in 2015. himti gathered from the study program of informatics engineering, information systems, information management and information technology education. Experts in the IT field continue to be born within the Himti organization. himti develops through education, training, practice, scientific development and certification held by local educational institutions. Every year, Himti students always get achievements in both academic and non-academic fields. solidarity and perseverance are the foundations of our organization.
